About us

MyRyd is web based application which allows to rent bicycles for daily commute. Anyone can access bicycles to commute as they want. Picking a bicycle from given pick up point near to customer. And can drop bicycle to nearest drop point to destination. In day to day life it's been expensive to ride motor vehicles. To cut off expense of middle class people and students this application will help it out.
